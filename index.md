**Privacy Policy for Skynie Movie Ticket Booking**

**Last Updated:** November 30, 2025

**1. Introduction**

Skynie Movie Booking (\"we\", \"our\", or \"us\") is committed to
protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect,
use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile
application.

By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

**2. Information We Collect**

**2.1 Personal Information**

When you create an account or use certain features, we may collect:

-   Name

-   Email address

-   Phone number

-   Profile avatar selection

**2.2 Usage Information**

We automatically collect:

-   Movie browsing activity (for recommendations)

-   Favorite movies

-   Booking and purchase history

-   Device information (model, OS version, unique identifiers)

-   App interaction data

**2.3 Payment Information**

-   Card payments are processed securely through third-party payment
    processors.

-   We **do not store full card details** on our servers.

-   Only masked card information (e.g., last 4 digits and card type) may
    be stored for convenience.

**3. How We Use Your Information**

We may use your information to:

-   Create and manage your user account

-   Process movie ticket bookings

-   Send booking confirmations, payment receipts, and e-tickets

-   Provide personalized movie suggestions

-   Improve app performance and user experience

-   Notify you about updates, promotions, or important service alerts

-   Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

**4. Data Storage and Security**

-   Your data is stored using **Firebase** (Google Cloud Platform).

-   All data transfers are encrypted using industry-standard protocols
    (HTTPS).

-   Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

-   We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to
    prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

**5. Third-Party Services**

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

-   **Firebase Authentication** -- secure login and identity management

-   **Firebase Realtime Database** -- storage of user and booking data

-   **Firebase Storage** -- storing profile images and related app media

These services are governed by their own privacy policies. We recommend
reviewing Google Firebase's Privacy Policy for more details.

**6. Your Rights**

You have the right to:

-   Access your personal data

-   Update or correct your information

-   Delete your account and associated data

-   Request a copy of your stored data

-   Opt out of non-essential promotional notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided
below.

**7. Data Retention**

We retain personal information as long as your account remains active.\
Upon account deletion, your associated data is permanently removed from
our servers except where retention is required by law or legitimate
business obligations.

**8. Children's Privacy**

Skynie Movie Booking is **not intended for children under the age of
13**.\
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under
13.\
If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

**9. Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.\
Any changes will be posted within the app along with an updated "Last
Updated" date.\
Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the revised
policy.
